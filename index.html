Mr. Pork Adobo

Popular Filipino Dish
One of the Most Delicious Dish

You can use pork. Other ingredients can be easily available. Its not complicated to prepare and cooking steps are easy..

Ingredients:

  • pork or chicken
  • ginger
  • shallots or onions
  • soy sauce

Ingredients:

  1. 1kg of pork or chicken
  2. 100mg of ginger
  3. 100mg of shallots or onions
  4. 100mg of soy sauce
